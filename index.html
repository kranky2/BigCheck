BigE's 5 Method —

Entry Checklist

Run before every trade. All mandatory items must pass.

0 / 8 checked 0 / 6 mandatory
● Mandatory — all must pass
1
TDI Cross confirmed
Green crossed Red — not just touching. Full cross only.
2
Candle timing — 1 or 2, within 10-15 mins
Candle 1 or 2 from the cross. Entry within 10-15 mins of candle open. If missed — skip.
3
Stoch K/D direction confirmed
Long: K above D. Short: K below D. Only valid between 20–80. If outside — ignore Stoch, TDI decides.
4
TDI angle
Long: 12–2 o'clock. Short: 4–6 o'clock. Borderline (2–4) = skip.
5
HA candle size + colour change
Decent sized body — no spinning tops, no wicks dominating. Colour must have changed on candle 1 or 2.
6
Economic calendar — no major red event
Check FF Calendar. If rate decision, NFP, CPI within this 4H candle window — skip. Wait for next candle after dust settles.
⚠️ Mark this FAIL if a red event is scheduled within the current 4H candle.
◐ Caution — flag if failing
7
S/R — room to run
Look left. If within 20–30 pips of clear support or resistance — skip or size down. EURJPY: watch 186.10–186.28.
8
TDI zone — not near 68 (longs) or 32 (shorts)
Move getting exhausted near the bands. Long near 68 or short near 32 — caution or skip.
◈ Context — good to have
9
Three Amigos alignment
If trading EUR/GBP/AUD — check the other two. If all three aligning, higher conviction. Pick the one with widest K/D gap and best candle.
10
Session + day context
Thursday 9pm into NFP Friday = high risk. London session = best momentum. Avoid 5M/15M/30M charts.
